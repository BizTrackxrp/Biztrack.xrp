Empower Your Supply Chain

BizTrack.xrp enables any business to issue on‑chain product IDs, generate QR codes and record supply chain events—without a centralized SaaS provider or onboarding process.

Create a Product ID

  1. Connect wallet: Use an XRPL‑compatible wallet (Xaman, GemWallet, Xaman, Ledger) to connect to our dApp.
  2. Enter details: Provide the product name, SKU and batch number, plus optional metadata like origin, ingredients or certifications.
  3. Mint token: A smart contract or XRPL Hook mints a unique on‑chain ID (NFT or token) representing the physical item.
  4. Download QR code: Print or embed the QR code on your product packaging to link customers to the blockchain record.

Record Supply Chain Events

At each stage—manufacturer, distributor, retailer—authorized wallets sign event updates such as Received at warehouse, Quality checked or Shipped to retailer. These events are stored on the XRP Ledger with optional rich metadata on IPFS or Arweave. Each update is immutable and publicly verifiable, ensuring transparency and authenticity.
