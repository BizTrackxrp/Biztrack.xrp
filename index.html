Why BizTrack?

Each product is assigned a unique identifier stored on the XRP Ledger. QR codes link the physical item to its blockchain record, enabling secure, tamper-proof tracking.

Supply chain updates are signed by authorized wallets and published on-chain. Optional metadata, such as photos or temperature logs, lives on decentralized storage like IPFS or Arweave.

Customers scan a QR code to view a product's verified history, authenticity and sustainability metrics. No crypto knowledge required - the landing page presents plain-language details.
