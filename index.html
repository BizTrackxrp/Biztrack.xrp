Why BizTrack?

Every product receives a tamper-evident ID on the XRPL. Updates are cryptographically signed by authorized wallets and verified in real-time by AI — so recalls are faster, fraud gets flagged sooner, and customers trust what they scan.

  • AI anomaly detection to catch duplicate IDs, missing events, and out-of-range telemetry before it reaches your customer.
  • XRPL notarization for each event: origin, custody, and sustainability attributes — signed and immutable.
  • One-scan confidence — a simple QR shows verified origin, authenticity, and key sustainability signals in plain language.
  • Drop-in onboarding — no heavy vendor lock-in; works with your existing labels & processes.
