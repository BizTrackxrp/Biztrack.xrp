Empower Your Supply Chain

BizTrack enables any business to issue on-chain product IDs, generate QR codes and record supply chain events—without a centralized SaaS provider or onboarding process.

Create a Product ID

  1. Connect wallet: Use an XRPL-compatible wallet (Xaman, GemWallet, Ledger) to connect to our dApp.
  2. Enter details: Provide the product name, SKU and batch number, plus optional metadata like origin or certifications.
  3. Mint token: A smart contract mints a unique on-chain ID (NFT or token) representing the physical item.
  4. Download QR code: Print or embed the QR code on your product packaging to link customers to the blockchain record.
